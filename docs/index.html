About

GPU Accalerated.

Open Darkroom Takes advantage of your computers Graphics Processor to edit you photo at full quality in real time.

Open Source

Anyone can View, Edit, or Contribute to Open Darkroom's Source Code.

Photo Processor

Open Darkroom is a Photo Processor, meaning it is meant to "develop" your photos, and make your photography look it's best. Not create illustrations and designs.

Requirements (Unvalidated)

Minimum

  • CPU: ~2Ghz, 2 Cores
  • GPU: 512MB of VRAM, OpenGL ES 3.0 / WebGL2 Support
  • RAM: 4GB+
  • OS: macOS (10.10+), Windows 10, or Linux

English: You need a Mac or PC made after 2012 with reasonable specs.

Recomended

  • CPU: ~3Ghz, 2 Cores
  • GPU: Dedicated Graphics Card with 2GB of VRAM
  • RAM: 6GB+

English: It's nice to have a decent computer made after 2016. A dedicated graphics card can go a long way.
